Reuse Methods

Here are some of the reusing techniques to improve sustainability

Wrapping Paper


Create your own wrapping paper! Wrap your gifts in the comic book pages from the Sunday paper, periodicals, or comics in your area. Children adore it, as do adults

Pen Holder


You can use your empty milk carton to make a pencil holder

Event


Insert a tree seedling within the bottom of a used milk carton. When planted, the carton will break down as the tree matures while shielding your seedling from the elements and pests.
